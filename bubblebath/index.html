about us

At BUBBLEBATH, we deeply value your well-being and we are committed to establish a trusting relationship with our beloved customers. Conveniently located at the heart of downtown, we offer a variety of different services for you to receive the best possible experience at our boutique.

COMMITMENT TO CUSTOMERS

BUBBLEBATH values our societyâ€™s major concerns on the topic of health and safety in our line of business. We always work towards achieving a level of cleanliness which exceeds governmental expectations. We make sure to keep the safety of our staff and clients as our main priority. We make sure to sanitize and purify our instruments for their health. Our commitment to hygiene is breathtaking as we constantly work towards providing an environment that our customers can feel comfort in.

  • We use Stainless Steel tools which are sterilized after each use.

  • After every single customer, we make sure to exchange our disposable nail files and buffers as we never reuse tools that cannot be sterilized.

  • When we use our wax, we never dip twice as it can cause irritation to the skin, as well as infections.

  • We always dispose of our gloves after each use as it can lead to a transfer of germs, as well as infections.
